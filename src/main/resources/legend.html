Legend:
RED = Stop was removed.
GREEN = Stop was added.
YELLOW = Stop time was changed.
+H:NN or +NN = stop was delayed by H hours and NN minutes
-H:NN or -NN = stop was advanced by H hours and NN minutes
WHITE = No change between schedules.
GRAY = No stop.
Stops column = change in trains stopping at that station (negative = that many fewer trains stopping, positive = that many more trains stopping)
